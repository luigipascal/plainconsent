Why PlainConsent?

Honest scope You list your scripts. We don’t pretend to auto-scan 500 trackers.
Free forever MIT license. No tiers, no page-count pricing, no surprise renewals.
Small-site first Made for marketing pages, indie products, and static sites — not ad-tech stacks.
Two-minute setup Drop in two files or one CDN snippet. Add a footer link. Done.

Install in 60 seconds

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://plainconsent.berta.one/dist/plainconsent.css" />
<script>
  window.plainConsentConfig = {
    privacyUrl: "/privacy.html",
    googleAnalyticsId: "G-XXXXXXXXXX",
    projectUrl: "https://plainconsent.berta.one",
  };
</script>
<script src="https://plainconsent.berta.one/dist/plainconsent.js" defer></script>

Add <a href="#" data-plainconsent-settings>Cookie settings</a> in your footer so visitors can change their choice.
